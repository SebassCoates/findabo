Be a Resource for Others

Submit your interests and contact information to be a resource for other students. By participating, you can help build a more involved community.

If you have various interests on campus, you can register multiple times!

Upon registering, the information above will be made visible to users of Find a Bo. You can ask to have your information removed at any time by emailing the current maintainer from your Tufts email.

Find a Bo is an open source project under GPL-3.0
